<p><strong>Steve : </strong>Up and out at a reasonable hour and on our way…</p>
<p>First requirement of the day was breakfast at A Pena and it was here that we experience our first <em>peregrino</em> traffic jam.&nbsp; The new influx of <em>peregrinos</em> at Sarria has overwhelmed the local bakery.&nbsp; We loiter for nearly an hour here.&nbsp; But the bread is very fresh and very good…</p>
